Tom Foolery — graphic designer, occasional printmaker tom@tomfoolery.test

studio the welcome screen

A one-person design studio for posters, books, and the occasional small website — based wherever the printer is open.

Tom is a graphic designer who works mostly with cultural and editorial clients. The studio runs at the pace of a small print shop: a handful of projects a year, each one carried from sketch through proof through final delivery by one set of hands.

For new work, a brief letter at tom@tomfoolery.test is the right entrypoint. For everything else, the four tiles to the left are the whole site.
